Our default sentiment dictionary in "defaultSentiments.txt" (which was transformed from the original file
 "subjclueslen1-HLTEMNLP05.tff" which is still in this package)
 is based on (but modified) from the following three works by Theresa Wilson:

1)Theresa Wilson. Mpqa opinion corpus. http://www.cs.pitt.edu/mpqa/.

2) Theresa Wilson, Janyce Wiebe, and Rebecca Hwa. Just how mad are you? Finding strong and weak opinion
clauses. AAAI, 2004.

3) Theresa Ann Wilson. Fine-grained Subjectivity and Sentiment Analysis: Recognizing the Intensity, Polarity,
and Attitudes of Private States. PhD thesis, University of Pittsburgh, 2008.

The list of contractions in "contractions.txt" is based on the following wikipedia page: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:List_of_English_contractions

The list of misspellings in "misspellings.txt" is based on the following wikipedia page: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Lists_of_common_misspellings/For_machines
